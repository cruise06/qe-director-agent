# Skill 07 AI Intelligence Radar

## Purpose
Track new AI technologies, products, and engineering practices, and assess their impact on quality engineering.

## Input
- Date
- AI news, product update, or technical change
- Short summary
- Why it may matter
- Quality engineering question to assess
- Expected output

## Output
- Conclusion
- Impact assessment
- Analysis
- Recommended actions
- Priority
- Director reminder

## Impact levels
- L1 Observe
- L2 Research
- L3 Pilot
- L4 Integrate into system

## Core analysis points
- What changed?
- Why does it matter to quality engineering?
- Does it affect quality gates, metrics, pilot projects, or AI quality standards?
- Should we observe, research, pilot, or integrate it?
- What concrete action should the director take?

# Rubric AI Impact Level

## Impact levels
- L1 Observe
- L2 Research
- L3 Pilot
- L4 Integrate into system

## L1 Observe
Use this when:
- The change is interesting but not yet actionable
- There is no immediate impact on quality gates, metrics, or workflow
- More signal is needed before action

## L2 Research
Use this when:
- The change may affect quality engineering soon
- The impact is plausible but not yet proven
- A focused analysis or short research task is needed

## L3 Pilot
Use this when:
- The change is relevant enough to validate in a pilot
- It may affect release control, testing strategy, or governance
- A limited-scope experiment is appropriate

## L4 Integrate into system
Use this when:
- The change already affects delivery or quality control
- It should enter quality gates, standards, metrics, or team workflow
- Delay would create governance or quality risk

## Required output
- Impact level
- Why it matters
- Recommended actions
- Priority
- Director reminder
